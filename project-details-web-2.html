Web-based POS Transmittal Enrollment System

The Web-based POS Transmittal Enrollment System was developed for Biliran Provincial Hospital to address the inefficiencies and lack of automation in the current manual process, which heavily relies on Excel. This outdated approach resulted in slow performance, especially in the processing of PMRFs and communication between the Malasakit Center and eClaims system. The new system was created to streamline and automate these operations for improved accuracy, speed, and security.

Key Features and Benefits of the Web-based POS Transmittal Enrollment System

  • Transmittal Generation: Automatically generates and compiles transmittal records for fast and reliable processing.
  • Patient Discharge Monitoring: Sorts and displays patients who are scheduled to be discharged soon for better preparation and management.
  • Performance Monitoring: Tracks user activity and performance to ensure accountability and efficiency.
  • PMRF Handling: Provides easy and accurate PMRF form filling, reducing errors and speeding up data input.
  • PMRF Export: Allows secure exporting of PMRF files for submission and archival purposes.
  • Data Security: Ensures that patient data is securely stored and accessed only by authorized users.
